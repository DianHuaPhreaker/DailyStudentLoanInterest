How To Calculate Daily Student Loan Interest

Calculate your daily interest rate (sometimes called interest rate factor).

Divide your annual student loan interest rate by the number of days in the year.

.07/365 = 0.00019, or 0.019%

Calculate the amount of interest your loan accrues per day.

Multiply your outstanding loan balance by your daily interest rate.

$10,000 x 0.00019 = $1.90

3. Find your montly interest payment

Multiply your daily interest amount by the number of days since your last payment.

$1.90 X 30 = $57
